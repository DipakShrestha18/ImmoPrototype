About

Award winning photographer Immo Klink graduated with a Master of the Laws then briefly joined Wolfgang Tillmans' studio in London. He applies his versatile style of image making and enquiries into social and political impact across the genres of fashion, editorial, art and commercial photography.

Magazines contributions include ADBUSTERS, Another Magazine, CAMERA, AUSTRIA, Dazed&Confused, EXIT, I-D, Jalouse, Independent Magazine, Liberation, L'OFFICIEL, New York Times Magazine, photoworks, Sueddeutsche Zeitung Magazine.

Immo's work is held in various public collections and has been exhibited in FOAM (Amsterdam), Museum of Contemporary Art Castilla y Leon (MUSAC National Portrait Gallery (London), Sala Rekalde (Bilbao), Northern Gallery of Contemporary Art (Sunderland), Museo del Xopo (Mexico City), The People's Museum (Manchester) and Victoria & Alber Museum (London) amongst others.

Immo has photographed and directed political campaigns with Benetton Unhate Foundation, Shelter Amenesty International and Brandalism/Barnbrook. Amongst his commericial commissions are campaigns for Levi's, Google and MC Saatchi.
